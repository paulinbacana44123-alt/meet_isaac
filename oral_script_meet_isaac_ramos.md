# Oral script — *Meet Isaac Ramos*

## Presentation length
Around **4 to 5 minutes**.

## Opening
Hello everyone.  
Today, instead of using a normal slide presentation, I decided to make a website to introduce and recommend a friend of mine for an au pair opportunity in Australia.  
His name is **Isaac de Souza Ramos**, and I think he would be a very interesting candidate for this role.

---

## Topic 1 — Basic facts
Isaac is **18 years old** and he studies **Computer Engineering**.  
He already has some professional experience because he worked as a **young apprentice and administrative assistant at LATAM**.  
At the moment, he is unemployed, but that means he is available for new opportunities and personal growth.

---

## Topic 2 — Personality
In terms of personality, Isaac is **energetic, funny, and sociable**.  
He is the kind of person who can make people feel comfortable very quickly.  
He is also **patient, dedicated, and hardworking**, which are very important qualities for someone who wants to live with a host family and help take care of children.

Of course, he is not perfect.  
He can be **a little silly, a little forgetful, and sometimes a bit impulsive**.  
However, I think these are small flaws, and they do not define him.  
In fact, they make him feel more real and human.

---

## Topic 3 — Why he would be a good au pair
I believe Isaac would be a good au pair for a few reasons.  
First, he has experience with children because he has a **younger brother**, so he already understands some of the responsibility involved in caring for someone younger.  
Second, he is very **charismatic and friendly**, which helps a lot when building trust with children and parents.  
Third, although his English is still improving, he already speaks **fairly well**, and I believe he can learn even faster in an international environment.

---

## Topic 4 — Interests and lifestyle
In his free time, Isaac likes **going out, going to church, and listening to music**.  
These hobbies show that he is social, active, and connected to values that can help him adapt well to family life.  
I think this is important because an au pair is not only someone who helps with tasks, but also someone who becomes part of the daily routine of a family.

---

## Final recommendation
For all these reasons, I would definitely recommend **Isaac de Souza Ramos** for an au pair opportunity in Australia.  
He is **responsible, hardworking, patient, and charismatic**.  
Even though he still has some things to improve, I believe he has the personality, the energy, and the potential to do a great job and grow a lot from this experience.

Thank you.

---

## Shorter emergency version
Hello everyone. Today I’m recommending my friend **Isaac de Souza Ramos** for an au pair opportunity in Australia. He is **18 years old**, studies **Computer Engineering**, and already has work experience as an administrative assistant at LATAM. He is **funny, sociable, patient, and hardworking**. He also has experience with children because he has a **younger brother**. In his free time, he likes **going out, going to church, and listening to music**. Although he still needs to improve his English, he already speaks fairly well and learns quickly. For these reasons, I believe he would be a very good au pair candidate. Thank you.

---
